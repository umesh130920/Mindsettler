About MindSettler

Our mission, vision, and approach towards psycho-education and mental wellness support.

Your Partner in Mental Wellness

MindSettler was founded on the belief that everyone deserves access to mental health education and personalized guidance. We focus on awareness, guidance, and personalized support in a safe and confidential environment.

Our approach combines evidence-based psycho-education with structured sessions that empower individuals to better understand their mental health and develop effective coping strategies.

Our Mission

To democratize mental health education and provide accessible, structured support that helps individuals understand and navigate their mental wellness journey with confidence.

Our Vision

A world where mental health education is accessible to all, and every individual has the tools and support needed to achieve emotional balance and resilience.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and processes

Sessions

What type of sessions do you offer?

We offer both online video sessions and in-person sessions at designated locations. Online sessions provide flexibility and convenience, while in-person sessions offer a more personal connection. All sessions are structured and led by qualified mental wellness guides.

How long is each session?

Our standard sessions are 50 minutes long. We also offer extended 75-minute sessions for more in-depth work and introductory 30-minute sessions for those who want to explore our approach before committing to a full session.

How many sessions will I need?

This varies based on individual needs and goals. After your initial assessment, we'll provide a recommendation. Some people benefit from just a few sessions to address specific concerns, while others prefer ongoing support. We'll work with you to determine what's most helpful for your situation.

Privacy & Confidentiality

Is my information kept confidential?

Absolutely. All client information is kept strictly confidential in accordance with professional standards and privacy laws. We use encrypted platforms for communication and secure storage for all records. Your privacy is our highest priority.

Are your sessions anonymous?

While we require basic information for scheduling and safety purposes, we offer the option to use a pseudonym during sessions if that makes you more comfortable. Our platform is designed to protect your identity while ensuring you receive quality support.

What happens in emergency situations?

While MindSettler focuses on psycho-education and wellness support, we have protocols for emergency situations. If there's imminent risk of harm to self or others, we're ethically obligated to take appropriate action to ensure safety. This is clearly outlined in our informed consent agreement.

Pricing & Payment

How much do sessions cost?

Our standard 50-minute session is $85. Extended sessions (75 minutes) are $120, and introductory sessions (30 minutes) are $45. We also offer package deals for multiple sessions. All prices are clearly listed before booking.

Do you accept insurance?

We are considered an out-of-network provider. We can provide you with a superbill to submit to your insurance company for potential reimbursement. We recommend checking with your insurance provider about their out-of-network mental health benefits.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and PayPal. Payment is collected at the time of booking to secure your appointment. For package deals, payment is required upfront for the entire package.

Booking Process

How do I book a session?

You can book a session directly through our website. Click the "Book Now" button, complete our brief intake form, select your preferred session type and time, and make your payment. You'll receive a confirmation email with all the details and any necessary links for online sessions.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Cancellations made with less than 24 hours notice will be charged 50% of the session fee. Missed appointments without notice will be charged the full session fee. We understand emergencies happen and review these cases individually.

Can I change my session type after booking?

Yes, you can change from online to in-person (or vice versa) with at least 24 hours notice, subject to availability. You can do this by contacting us through your booking confirmation email or through your client portal.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2023

MindSettler collects information to provide better services to all our users. We collect information in the following ways:

  • Information we get from your use of our services: We collect information about the services that you use and how you use them.
  • Session information: We maintain records of your session bookings, preferences, and wellness journey progress.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect from all our services to provide, maintain, protect and improve them, to develop new ones, and to protect MindSettler and our users. We also use this information to offer you tailored content.

3. Information Security

We work hard to protect MindSettler and our users from unauthorized access to or unauthorized alteration, disclosure or destruction of information we hold. In particular:

  • We encrypt many of our services using SSL

4. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. You can also object to our processing of your personal information, ask us to restrict processing of your personal information or request portability of your personal information.

5. Changes

Our Privacy Policy may change from time to time. We will post any privacy policy changes on this page and, if the changes are significant, we will provide a more prominent notice.

Non-Refund Policy

Effective Date: December 2023

1. Session Payments

All payments for MindSettler sessions are final and non-refundable. Once a session has been booked and payment has been processed, the amount paid is not eligible for refund under any circumstances.

2. Cancellation Policy

While we do not offer refunds, we do allow session rescheduling according to the following terms:

  • 24+ hours notice: Sessions can be rescheduled at no charge with at least 24 hours notice
  • Less than 24 hours notice: Sessions rescheduled with less than 24 hours notice will be charged 50% of the session fee
  • No show: Sessions missed without any notice will be charged the full session fee

3. Package Sessions

For clients who purchase session packages:

  • All package sales are final and non-refundable
  • Unused sessions in a package do not expire and can be used at any time
  • Package sessions are transferable between online and offline formats
  • Packages cannot be transferred to another individual

4. Exceptional Circumstances

In truly exceptional circumstances (medical emergencies, natural disasters, etc.), we may offer session credits at our discretion. These credits will be valid for 6 months from the date of issue.

5. Agreement

By booking a session with MindSettler, you acknowledge and agree to this Non-Refund Policy. We recommend reviewing your schedule carefully before booking to ensure you can attend at the selected time.

Confidentiality Policy

Required Acknowledgement Before First Session

1. Confidentiality Commitment

MindSettler is committed to protecting your privacy and maintaining the confidentiality of all information shared during our sessions. Everything discussed in our sessions is strictly confidential.

2. What We Protect

We protect all information including but not limited to:

  • Personal identification information
  • Session content and discussions
  • Wellness assessments and progress notes
  • Payment information
  • Communication records

3. Limitations to Confidentiality

There are specific situations where we are legally and ethically required to break confidentiality:

  • Imminent risk of harm: If we believe you are at imminent risk of harming yourself or others
  • Child or vulnerable adult abuse: Suspected or disclosed abuse of children or vulnerable adults
  • Court order: If we receive a court order or subpoena
  • Supervision: Your case may be discussed anonymously in professional supervision

4. Data Security Measures

We implement multiple security measures to protect your information:

  • Encrypted communication platforms for online sessions
  • Secure, password-protected electronic records
  • Limited access to confidential information
  • Regular security audits and updates

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your session records
  • Request corrections to your information
  • Know who has accessed your records
  • File a complaint if you believe your confidentiality has been breached

Note: This acknowledgement is required before your first session can proceed. Your guide will verify this agreement during your initial consultation.
